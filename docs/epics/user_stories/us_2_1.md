# US-2.1 – Enter Amount and Receive Cash

## Card

As a **Customer**, I **MUST** enter a withdrawal amount and receive cash from the CashDispenser so that I can obtain physical currency from my Account.

## Conversation

- This is the happy-path withdrawal: amount entry → Transaction creation → Card ejection → cash dispensing.
- The Bank Backend creates a Transaction with `transactionType = WITHDRAWAL` and `status = PENDING`, debits the Account. The ATM ejects the Card before dispensing cash, and on successful dispensing updates the Transaction to `COMPLETED`.
- The ATM System updates CashCassette quantities and the Bank Backend writes an AuditLog entry.
- Corresponds to activity diagram steps in [Withdraw Cash](../../use_case/withdraw_cash/withdrawCash.md): "Customer enters withdrawal amount" → all checks pass → "Bank Backend creates Transaction" → "ATM ejects Card" → "ATM dispenses cash" → "Bank Backend updates Transaction (status = COMPLETED)".
- KANO: **Basic** — cash withdrawal is the primary reason a Customer uses an ATM.

## Confirmation

- **Given** the Customer has an active Session and sufficient Account balance, the amount is within `dailyLimit`, and the CashDispenser has sufficient cash
  **When** the Customer enters a withdrawal amount
  **Then** the Bank Backend creates a Transaction (`PENDING`), debits the Account, the ATM ejects the Card, the ATM dispenses cash, the Transaction is updated to `COMPLETED`, CashCassette quantities are adjusted, and an AuditLog entry is written.

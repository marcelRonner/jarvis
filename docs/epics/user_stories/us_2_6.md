# US-2.6 – Eject Card Before Cash Dispensing

## Card

As a **Customer**, I **MUST** receive my Card back from the ATM before cash is dispensed so that I do not forget to take my Card.

## Conversation

- After all pre-checks pass and the Bank Backend creates a Transaction with `status = PENDING`, the ATM ejects the Card **before** dispensing cash.
- This prevents the common usability problem of Customers forgetting their Card once they have cash in hand.
- The Session remains `ACTIVE` after Card ejection — the Customer can still choose another transaction or print a Receipt.
- Applies to the withdrawal path only (Business Process step 4a.6). Non-withdrawal transaction types eject the Card at Session close (step 5.5).
- Corresponds to activity diagram step in [Withdraw Cash](../../use_case/withdraw_cash/withdrawCash.md): "ATM ejects Card" (between Transaction creation and cash dispensing).
- KANO: **Basic** — prevents a high-impact usability failure that erodes trust in the ATM.

## Confirmation

- **Given** the Customer has an active Session, all withdrawal pre-checks have passed, and a Transaction with `status = PENDING` has been created
  **When** the ATM is about to dispense cash
  **Then** the ATM ejects the Card first, waits for the Customer to retrieve it, and only then dispenses cash from the CashDispenser.

- **Given** the Customer has completed a withdrawal (Card already ejected)
  **When** the Customer chooses "another transaction" at step 5.4
  **Then** the Session remains `ACTIVE` and the Customer can re-insert the Card to continue.

- **Given** a non-withdrawal transaction (balance inquiry or transfer) is completed
  **When** the Session ends at step 5.5
  **Then** the Card is ejected at Session close as before (no early ejection).

# US-5.2 – Decline Receipt Printing

## Card

As a **Customer**, I **WOULD LIKE** to decline Receipt printing so that I can complete my Session faster and reduce paper waste.

## Conversation

- After any completed Transaction the ATM asks whether the Customer wants a Receipt.
- If the Customer declines, no Receipt is generated and the flow proceeds directly to the "Another transaction?" prompt.
- Corresponds to activity diagram steps in [Print Receipt](../../use_case/print_receipt/printReceipt.md): "Customer wants a Receipt?" → (no).
- KANO: **Attractive** — the option to skip printing is a convenience that delights environmentally conscious Customer without causing dissatisfaction if absent.

## Confirmation

- **Given** a Transaction with status `COMPLETED` exists in the current Session
  **When** the Customer chooses not to print a Receipt
  **Then** no Receipt is generated and the ATM proceeds to the next step.

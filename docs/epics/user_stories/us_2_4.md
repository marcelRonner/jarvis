# US-2.4 – Check CashDispenser Availability

## Card

As a **Customer**, I **NEED** to be informed when the CashDispenser has insufficient cash so that I understand why the withdrawal cannot proceed.

## Conversation

- After balance verification the ATM System checks `CashDispenser.totalCashAvailable` and CashCassette quantities.
- If the CashDispenser cannot fulfil the requested amount, the ATM displays "ATM out of cash" and returns to transaction selection.
- No Transaction is created.
- Corresponds to activity diagram steps in [Withdraw Cash](../../use_case/withdraw_cash/withdrawCash.md): "CashDispenser has sufficient cash?" → (no) → "Display 'ATM out of cash'".
- KANO: **Basic** — the Customer expects a clear message rather than a silent failure.

## Confirmation

- **Given** the Account has sufficient balance and the amount is within `dailyLimit`, but the CashDispenser does not have enough cash
  **When** the ATM System checks CashDispenser availability
  **Then** the ATM displays "ATM out of cash" and no Transaction is created.

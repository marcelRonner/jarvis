# US-1.3 – Retry PIN on Invalid Entry

## Card

As a **Customer**, I **NEED** to re-enter my PIN when it is invalid so that I can correct a typing mistake without restarting the entire authentication.

## Conversation

- The ATM allows the Customer to retry PIN entry up to the maximum number of attempts.
- Each failed attempt decrements the remaining attempt counter.
- The ATM displays a clear message that the PIN was incorrect and shows how many attempts remain.
- Corresponds to activity diagram steps in [Authenticate](../../use_case/authenticate/authenticate.md): "PIN invalid?" → "Attempts remaining?" → "Customer re-enters PIN".
- KANO: **Basic** — preventing PIN typos from causing Card retention is an expected safety net.

## Confirmation

- **Given** the Customer has entered an incorrect PIN and attempts are remaining
  **When** the Customer re-enters the PIN
  **Then** the Bank Backend validates the new PIN, and if correct the ATM creates a Session with status `ACTIVE`.

- **Given** the Customer has entered an incorrect PIN and attempts are remaining
  **When** the Customer re-enters an incorrect PIN again
  **Then** the remaining attempt count decreases and the Customer is prompted once more.
